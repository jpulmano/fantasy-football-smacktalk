Welcome to Fantasy Football smack talk!

# How to setup
- Initialize .env
- You will need the following details:

LEAGUE_ID
- found by visiting https://fantasy.espn.com/football/, navigating to your league, and pulling the leagueId from the URL

YEAR
- the current year

SWID and ESPN_S2
- these are the cookies that allow you to authenticate
- navigate to espn fantasy website on chrome
- right click > inspect
- Application > cookies > fantasy.espn.com
- get ESPN_S2 and SWID

Get your LLM API keys from each source.
